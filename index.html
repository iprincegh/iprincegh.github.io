about me/

Prince Oppong Boakye is a Geoinformatics and Spatial Data Science researcher specializing in spatial epidemiology, human mobility modeling, and infectious disease risk mapping. My work integrates multi-source population flow datasets with Bayesian spatiotemporal modeling to understand disease transmission dynamics at fine spatial scales.
